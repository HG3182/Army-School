Admission Procedure and Admission Test

Details on the admission process, including test information and required criteria.

Priorities for Admission

The priorities for admission, subject to seat availability, are as follows:

  • Children of serving Army personnel including DSC (Ex-Army), children of Army widows, and children of TA personnel with a minimum of 5 years embodied service.
  • Children of Ex-Army personnel retired/invalided out on medical grounds with pension, DSC personnel retired with pension from DSC.
  • Children of serving Air Force, Naval, and Coast Guard personnel.
  • Children of retired Air Force, Naval, and Coast Guard personnel.
  • Children of Ex-Army personnel who left the Army with less than 10 years of service and are not categorized as ESM.
  • Children of civilians paid out of Defence Estimates, CAPF, MES, GREF, and TA (when not embodied).
  • Grandchildren of serving/retired Army Personnel.
  • Other children.

Admission Based on Transfer Certificate

The following categories of children may be admitted based on a Transfer Certificate from the previous school, without an admission test:

  • Children coming on Pass/Promoted TC from other APS/Army School/KV between 01 Apr to 31 Jul.
  • Children who studied for 10 academic months in another APS/Army School/KV and are seeking admission after 31 Jul.

An evaluation test may be conducted for these children to assist in section allocation.

Important Notes
  • Incomplete applications will not be accepted.
  • Please ensure that names match the documents exactly.
  • No corrections/changes in name or date of birth will be entertained after submission.
  • If caste is mentioned, a valid caste certificate must be attached.
  • Attach the Permanent Education Number with the admission form.
