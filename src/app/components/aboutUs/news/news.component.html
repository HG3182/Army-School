Southern Command Level Ethics Bowl Competition: 01 Aug 2022

The 1st of August 2022 witnessed the final tussle between APS Mumbai and APS Golconda, competing for the finals of the Ethics Bowl Competition. The event began with a warm welcome from ARMY PUBLIC SCHOOL Khadki , followed by Vandana, a traditional dance recital. The program was inaugurated by Mrs. Anita Nain, Regional President AWWA, SOUTHERN STAR, along with other dignitaries including Mrs. Anita Walia, Regional Vice President AWWA, SOUTHERN STAR, Mrs. Arti Jhaldial, CHAIRPERSON SCHOOLS, SOUTHERN STAR, Col Anupam Mathur, Dir AWES Southern Command, Brig DG Patwardhan, and others.

The judges for the event were Mrs. Pooja Anand, a social activist and politician, Mr. Aglawe, an eminent lawyer, and Dr. Pranay Oswal, a successful entrepreneur. The moderator was Mrs. Poonam Thakur. Both teams showcased impressive argumentative skills, making the event an engaging learning experience for all present.

After a challenging three-hour competition, APS Golconda emerged as the winner. The event concluded with a vote of thanks by Mrs. Rupan Jaswal, Vice Principal of APS Khadki , and a message encouraging students to believe in their dreams.
