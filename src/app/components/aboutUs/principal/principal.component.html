Principal's Message

A message from the Principal of Army Public School Kirkee

Welcome to Army Public School!

As the Principal of Army Public School Kirkee, I am proud to lead a vibrant and dynamic educational community that focuses on nurturing the potential of every student. Our dedicated and passionate staff are committed to providing a nurturing and inclusive learning environment that promotes academic excellence, personal growth, and character development.

At APS Kirkee, we believe in providing an all-rounded education that encompasses not only academic achievements but also the development of essential life skills. Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to meet the diverse needs and interests of our students, ensuring they are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to thrive in an ever-changing world.

We foster a culture of collaboration, respect, and empathy, encouraging our students to become confident and responsible global citizens. We provide a range of exciting Co-curricular activities and Houses that allow students to explore their interests, discover their talents, and develop their leadership skills.

We also recognize the importance of parent involvement in a child’s education. We value the partnership between home and school and invite parents to actively engage with us in their child’s learning journey. Together, we can provide the best possible education and support, ensuring the success and well-being of our students.

I am honored to serve as the Principal of Army Public School Kirkee and I look forward to working closely with all stakeholders to create a safe and inclusive learning environment where every student can thrive.

Mrs. Rupan Jaswal
Principal, Army Public School Kirkee
